Lycanthrobia

Game Concept

The game takes place in a forest in the nighttime. Your character is a hunter and a skilled tracker who was out in the forest and attacked by a never before seen creature. Your character loses his gun and needs to escape. However, your character’s tracking and hunting knowledge gives him the ability to be aware of his scent and mask it temporarily using mud. The more he exerts himself, the stronger his scent will become until the wolf will be onto him immediately, so before his scent becomes to strong, he needs to find a way to dampen his scent, which will wear off eventually. He is also skilled at hiding, but it will only work if his scent is weak enough. Your goal is to navigate around the forest, searching for items to your advantage, like diverting the creatures’s attention away from you.
